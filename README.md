# Netflix Customer Churn Analysis

Analyzing customer behavior, subscription patterns, and churn drivers to identify retention opportunities and reduce revenue loss using Excel, SQL Server, and Power BI.

## 📌 Project Overview

The Netflix Customer Churn Analysis project aims to identify the key factors influencing customer churn by analyzing customer demographics, subscription plans, engagement behavior, satisfaction levels, and payment-related issues.

The project helps uncover customer retention challenges and provides actionable insights to improve customer loyalty and business performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Subscription-based businesses like Netflix rely heavily on customer retention for sustainable growth.

High customer churn can result in:

* Revenue loss
* Reduced customer lifetime value
* Increased customer acquisition costs
* Lower customer satisfaction
* Decreased business profitability

The objective is to identify why customers leave the platform and recommend strategies to improve retention.

## 💡 Key Insights

* The overall churn rate reached **57.03%**, indicating significant customer retention challenges.
* A total of **14,258 customers** discontinued their subscriptions.
* The **Premium Plan** recorded the highest number of churned customers.
* Customers in the **Adult Age Category** experienced the highest churn.
* **Australia** contributed the highest number of churned customers.
* Lower satisfaction scores were strongly associated with higher churn rates.
* Customers with multiple payment failures showed a significantly greater likelihood of churning.
* Customer churn resulted in an estimated revenue loss of **6,186,092**.

* Launch personalized retention campaigns.

### Strengthen Regional Retention Strategies

* Investigate churn patterns in high-risk countries.
* Deliver localized content and marketing campaigns.
